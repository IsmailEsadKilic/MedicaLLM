At the beginning of Second Semester, each team is required to submit a Plan Report.

This report builds upon the MVP developed and presented at the end of Semester 1. Its purpose is to critically evaluate the current MVP, identify its limitations, and present a clear and realistic plan for completing and validating the final system during Semester 2.

The report should include:

A brief summary of the project and MVP

Evaluation of the MVP and its limitations

Clearly defined objectives for Semester 2

Proposed system enhancements and updated design

Technical and implementation plan

Evaluation and validation strategy

Timeline, milestones, and risk analysis

📌 Submission deadline: End of Week 4
📊 Weight: 40% of the final course grade

Please have it signed by your customer and submit the hard copy by the end of the fifth week.

This is a planning-oriented report. Final results or completed implementations are not expected at this stage.
